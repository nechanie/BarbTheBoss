Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Barb The Boss ("Bot"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share data when you interact with the Bot on Discord.

2. Information We Collect

  • Discord User ID: To associate your account and provide personalized features.
  • Server ID: To manage server-specific settings and clan registrations.
  • Clan Tags & Data: Information you submit via commands (e.g., clan tags) to fetch Clash of Clans data.
  • Preferences: Notification and scheduling settings you configure.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Link Discord accounts to Clash of Clans accounts.
  • Send real-time war notifications and attack reminders.
  • Store and apply your preferences for default clans and schedules.
  • Improve and maintain the Bot’s functionality.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information:

  • With Discord, to operate the Bot under their platform rules.
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • With service providers who perform services on our behalf (e.g., hosting).

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the Bot’s features and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us (see Section 8).

6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Bot is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the Bot after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or requests regarding your data, please contact us at ethan.nechanicky@gmail.com.
